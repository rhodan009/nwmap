## Show influence map

On [Aeternum Map](https://aeternum-map.gg/?section=influences), you can find the influence maps per server.  
You can contribute by scanning the factions influence map on your server with the [Aeternum Map app](https://www.overwolf.com/app/Leon_Machens-Aeternum_Map). The overlay is visible in-game and usable with a simple click.
